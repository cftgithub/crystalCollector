Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. clicking on a crystl will add a specific amount of points to your total score. When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score. The value of each crystal will be hidden until you click on it. You win if you your total score matches the random number. If your score goes above the random number, you lose.
